How To

Simply add a URL to the URL field and name the entry before clicking Submit. These are the tabs that will open when SFW is executed.
To open the SFW window, press Ctrl+M. To remove a tab from SFW, click the Show URLs button and click the x to the right of the site name. It won't open in the Window next time.
SFW syncs to your Google account. If not signed in, SFW uses local storage instead.
